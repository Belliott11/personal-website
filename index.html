About

I am a political science student at Drexel University in the Honors Program, with academic interests in democratic theory and international relations. My work focuses on understanding how political systems function and how public opinion is shaped in an increasingly polarized environment.

I currently intern with the campaign for D'Angelo Virgo for Pennsylvania House of Representatives District 192, where I gain hands-on experience in political organizing, outreach, and campaign strategy.

In addition to my internship, I am preparing to conduct research through Drexel’s STAR Scholars program. My proposed project examines the relationship between political polarization and film in the 21st century, analyzing how media both reflects and influences ideological divides.
